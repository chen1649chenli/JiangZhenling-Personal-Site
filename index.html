Zhenling Jiang

Welcome to my webpage! I am a PhD candidate in quantitative marketing at the John M. Olin School of Business at Washington University in St. Louis. My research focuses on consumer finance, behavioral economics and search model. I am advised by professor Tat Y. Chan. Prior to joining Olin, I worked at Bose for five years as a market researcher, doing consumer analysis. I received my bachelor's degree from Nankai University and master's degree from Brandies University, both in Economics.
